Privacy Policy of Cookee

We take the protection of your data seriously. This privacy policy informs you about the data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What data do we collect?

  • Personal data such as your email address provided during registration via Firebase Auth.
  • App data such as your saved recipes and associated images.
  • Technical data required for the operation and security of the app.

How do we use your data?

  • For authentication and management of your account.
  • To store and display your own recipes and images.
  • For management and improvement of the app by the operator.

Who has access to the data?

Only you as a user can view your own recipes and images when logged in. The app operator has admin access to all data in Firebase for management purposes. No data is shared with third parties, except Firebase as a technical service provider.

Data storage and security

Data is securely stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage. Firestore Security Rules and authentication ensure that only authorized persons have access.

Your rights

  • Access to your stored data.
  • Request deletion or modification of your data.
