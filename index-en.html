Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How can I add my own recipes? → Go to the home screen, scroll left, and tap the "+" icon.
  • What is recipe effort? → Recipe effort combines components like cooking times and the number of ingredients into a single rating. You can filter by this effort on the search page.
  • What are components? → Components are grouped parts of a recipe, such as a sauce or a dough. In the cooking steps, you can select how to further process each component.
